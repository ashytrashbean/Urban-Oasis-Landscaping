# Urban Oasis Landscaping: A "Client" Simulation Project
This project was built to practice my ability to translate a business pitch into a professional website. 

I tasked an AI to pitch me a mock business with specific needs, and I acted as the "Lead Developer" to bring it to life.

## The Pitch

Urban Oasis Landscaping: "We design and install modern, minimalist city gardens. We need a sleek portfolio website that uses large, high-resolution before-and-after photo galleries to demonstrate our skill in transforming small spaces.".
